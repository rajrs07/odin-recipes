Ground Beef

Today, we are gonna cook some ground beef in bulk quantity as a meal prep for the whole week.

Ingredients

  • 1.2 kg Ground Beef
  • salt
  • pepper
  • Seasoning of your choice
  • Cast iron skillet (optional)
  1. Heat up some oil in your pan until it extremely hot.
  2. Drop your ground beef into the pan without breaking it up and let it sit for about 2-3 minutes to get the browning effect.
  3. Break apart the beef into smaller chunks and drop in your salt, pepper and seassoning of your choice according to your taste.
  4. Stir the ground beef after every minute for about 5 minutes.
  5. Break apart a bigger chunk of beef and ensure it is not pink to ensure it is cooked. You could also use a meat thermometer, cooked ground beef has an internal temperature of about 165 degrees celsius.
  6. Let it cool for about 5 minutes and your delicious ground beef is ready to serve.
