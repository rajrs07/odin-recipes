Spicy Chicken Breast (Chilli Chicken Seasoning)

Today, we are gonna bake some juicy and delicious chicken breasts that are also healthy.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breasts
  • Chilli Sauce
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Ketchup
  • Soy Sauce
  • Olive Oil
  • Plain Yogurt
  1. Coat the chicken breasts with oil and season with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat up your oven to 425 degrees and put in the chicken breasts for about 13 minutes each side depending on their thickness.
  3. After both sides have been cooked, you can also check with a meat thermometer. The cooked tempereature for chicken breasts is also 165 degrees celsius. Take the chicken breasts out of the oven.
  4. Coat a pan with olive oil and heat up a mix of yogurt, chilli sauce, ketchup and soy sauce. You can add butter to thicken up your sauce.
  5. Once the sauce is heated up, you can put in your chicken breasts into the sauce for about a minute each side or until sufficiently coated.
  6. Let them cool for about 5 minutes and they are ready to feast and lock in your gains!
