Alfredo Pasta

Today we are gonna cook some saucy and delicious alfredo pasta.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Heavy Cream
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • pepper
  • Italian Seasoning
  1. Take your serving size of pasta around 150 grams.
  2. Heat up water in a pan to a boil and throw in your pasta and season the boiled water generously with salt.
  3. Let pasta cook for about 10 minutes and stir occasionally
  4. Drain the water after 10 minutes and pour in some heavy cream into your pasta on low heat.
  5. Put in chunks of butter into your sauce. Please note, more butter will make your sauce thick so add it according to your preference.
  6. Season the sauce with salt, pepper and italian seasoning generously
  7. Mix the sauce and pasta by stirring and let it heat until it the sauce comes to the consistency of your preference.
  8. That's it! Your delicious alfredo pasta is now ready.
